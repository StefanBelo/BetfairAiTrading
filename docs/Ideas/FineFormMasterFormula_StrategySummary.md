# The Fine Form Master Formula – Betting Strategy Ideas

## Core Principles
- Use a rigid, rule-based system for selections to avoid emotional decision-making and maintain discipline.
- The Fine Form Master Formula rates horses based on:
  - First 3 in the betting forecast
  - Last 2 outings in the current season
  - Course and distance winners
  - Top 3 from handicap ratings
- Maximum rating is 20, with additional marks for joint ratings.

## Key Filters to Improve Strike Rate
- Avoid races with extreme going (ground conditions).
- Only consider races of class 4 or higher.
- Select races where most runners have winning form in their last two outings.
- At least 80% of the field should have a handicap rating.
- Maximum field size: 12 runners.

## Value Betting
- Only bet when the offered price is greater than the true chance of the horse winning (positive expected value).
- Fewer runners in a race generally offer better value due to lower bookmaker overround.
- Avoid betting when the price is too short.

## Staking Strategies
- Level stakes are recommended unless the system is proven profitable.
- Kelly Criterion is discussed for optimal staking, but cautioned due to the difficulty of accurately estimating true probabilities in horse racing.
- Suggested staking plan:
  - Start with a 2-point stake.
  - Increase stake by 1 point after each loss.
  - After a win at odds-on, keep the same stake.
  - After a win at 1/1 to 11/8, reduce stake by 2 points.
  - After a win at 6/4 or longer, reduce stake by 4 points.
  - Minimum stake is 2 points.

## Probability and Expected Value
- Use statistical facts, not subjective views, to assign probabilities.
- Only back selections with a positive expected value (EV).

## Betfair Market Insights
- Betfair markets often trade close to a 100% book, but value can be found by comparing implied odds from matched amounts to offered odds.
- Price disparity (offered odds much greater than implied odds) does not always mean value; layers may be taking on risky horses.

## Practical Takeaways for Betfair Trading
- Develop a selection system based on objective criteria and filters.
- Focus on races with fewer runners and higher class.
- Use expected value calculations to guide betting decisions.
- Consider level stakes or cautious variable staking based on strike rate and bank size.
- Analyze Betfair market data for price disparities, but validate value with additional criteria.
